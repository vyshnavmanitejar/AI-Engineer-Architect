## Overview

This module serves as a comprehensive introduction to the foundational concepts and principles of **Artificial Intelligence**. It explores the terminology, application domains, and core ideas that underpin modern AI systems, providing you with a solid foundation for advanced study in machine learning and deep learning.

---

## 📚 Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- ✅ Understand the fundamental concepts and definitions of Artificial Intelligence
- ✅ Explore key AI terminology and industry-specific concepts
- ✅ Identify diverse application domains for AI technologies
- ✅ Recognize the significance and impact of AI in modern society
- ✅ Distinguish between AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning
- ✅ Analyze real-world AI applications across different industries
